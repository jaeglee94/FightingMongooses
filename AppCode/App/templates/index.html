Airline Key Players Analysis

American Airlines, Delta, and Southwest are the world's three largest airlines in terms of number of passengers carried . YOY since 2005, their revenues have steadily increased. Most recently, American Airlines' revenue just surpassed Delta's and quarterly earnings show that they continue to outperform. This dashboard presents a deeper dive into financial trends and analytics on domestic routes (within the US) for each of the airlines to identify areas of opportunity against competitors.
